# Success as taught in the Quran & Sunnah

## Straight from the source:

### 1. Qur'an 26:88–89
**Quote:** "The Day when neither wealth nor children will benefit anyone, except one who comes to Allah with a sound heart."

**Authenticity:** ✅ Verified - Qur'an, Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:88-89)

**Arabic text (approximate):**
يَوْمَ لَا يَنفَعُ مَالٌ وَلَا بَنُونَ إِلَّا مَنْ أَتَى اللَّهَ بِقَلْبٍ سَلِيمٍ

---

### 2. Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim 2564
**Quote:** "Allah does not look at your appearance or your wealth, but He looks at your hearts and your deeds."

**Authenticity:** ✅ Verified - Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim, Hadith 2564

**Note:** This is a well-known authentic hadith from Sahih Muslim.

---

### 3. Qur'an 87:14–15
**Quote:** "Successful is the one who purifies himself, remembers the name of his Lord, and prays."

**Authenticity:** ✅ Verified - Qur'an, Surah Al-A'la (87:14-15)

**Arabic text (approximate):**
قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن تَزَكَّىٰ وَذَكَرَ اسْمَ رَبِّهِ فَصَلَّىٰ

---

### 4. Qur'an 91:9–10
**Quote:** "Successful is the one who purifies it, and ruined is the one who corrupts it."

**Authenticity:** ✅ Verified - Qur'an, Surah Ash-Shams (91:9-10)

**Arabic text (approximate):**
قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن زَكَّاهَا وَقَدْ خَابَ مَن دَسَّاهَا

---

### 5. Qur'an 13:28
**Quote:** "Unquestionably, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest."

**Authenticity:** ✅ Verified - Qur'an, Surah Ar-Ra'd (13:28)

**Arabic text (approximate):**
الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَتَطْمَئِنُّ قُلُوبُهُم بِذِكْرِ اللَّهِ ۗ أَلَا بِذِكْرِ اللَّهِ تَطْمَئِنُّ الْقُلُوبُ

---

### 6. Qur'an 2:201
**Quote:** "Our Lord, give us in this world good and in the Hereafter good, (...)."

**Authenticity:** ✅ Verified - Qur'an, Surah Al-Baqarah (2:201)

**Arabic text (approximate):**
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

---

### 7. Qur'an 53:42
**Quote:** "And that the ultimate goal is (going back) to your Lord"

**Authenticity:** ✅ Verified - Qur'an, Surah An-Najm (53:42)

**Arabic text (approximate):**
وَأَنَّ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ الْمُنتَهَىٰ

---
